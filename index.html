At Oak Frame Studio, we create handmade oak structures meticulously designed to complement individual homes, gardens and outdoor spaces. What We Create.

Oak Porches

A handcrafted oak porch elevates both the appearance and function of a home's entrance. It provides welcoming shelter from the elements, creates a more inviting approach and adds lasting character to the property. Whether you prefer something simple and understated or richly detailed and distinctive, the right design can be shaped around different architectural styles, spaces and budgets, while still feeling in harmony with the home, working beautifully in everyday use and adding genuine long term value.

Garden Structures: Pergolas, Garages & Sheds

Oak framed garden structures bring shape, purpose and visual appeal to an outdoor space, while making it more usable throughout the year.
